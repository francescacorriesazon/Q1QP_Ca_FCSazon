Have you ever wondered about the current state of the animals in the wild? Have you ever been curious to know how many of them are still running around free to enjoy the wonders of nature? If so, then this Statistics project might just be the one to answer some of your questions about wildlife. Our project aims to educate the viewers about the present conditions of certain wild animals around Asia. Furthermore, we also talk about some physical characteristics and other facts about these animals.
